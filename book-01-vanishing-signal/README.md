# Book 1 — The Vanishing Signal

**The Architectures of Memory**, Book 1. *Why Networks Cannot Remember, and What That Reveals About Mind.*

This volume traces how early neural networks promised general learning machines, how backpropagation revived the field, and how recurrent models exposed a structural limit: signals that vanish across time. The same pattern is read through organizations and philosophy — proof of failure as a form of knowledge.
